Frequently asked

Do I need an account to try the score?

No. Connect any wallet on the dashboard and you get a free score instantly. An account only unlocks history, alerts, and multi-wallet linking.

How is billing handled?

All payments go through Stripe Checkout — we never see your card. Your subscription auto-renews monthly and you can cancel from the customer portal at any time.

What happens if I downgrade or cancel?

Your paid features stay active until the end of the current billing period, then your account drops to the Free tier. Your history and linked wallets are preserved.

Do you offer annual pricing?

Annual plans (~2 months free) and team seats are available on Plus and Enterprise. Email us.

Is the public API really free?

Yes. The 300–850 score endpoint is free with generous rate limits. Paid tiers raise the cap and unlock authenticated endpoints (history, alerts, RWA).

Do you take crypto?

Stripe-supported cards today; ETH / USDC checkout is on the roadmap for Plus and Enterprise.
