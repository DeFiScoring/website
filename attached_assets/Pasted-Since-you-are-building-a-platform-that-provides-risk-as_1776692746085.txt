### 3\. Inherent Risks of Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

By using this Website, you acknowledge that participating in Decentralized Finance involves extreme risk, including but not limited to:

  * **Regulatory Risk:** Changes in global regulations may impact the legality or functionality of certain protocols.
  * **Oracle Failures:** Data feeds used by protocols can fail or be manipulated, leading to liquidations or loss of funds.
