### 1. The Scoring Algorithm ($H_s$)
To make this high-value, the score shouldn't just be about balance; it should be about **reputation**. We can define the Health Score ($H_s$) as:

$$H_s = (0.4 \times L_r) + (0.3 \times LP_v) + (0.2 \times G_p) + (0.1 \times A_g)$$

* **$L_r$ (Loan Reliability):** Successful repayments vs. liquidations on Aave/Compound.
* **$LP_v$ (Liquidity Provision):** Total value and duration of liquidity provided.
* **$G_p$ (Governance Participation):** Votes cast on Snapshot or Tally.
* **$A_g$ (Account Age):** How long the wallet has been active on-chain.
