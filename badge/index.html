How it works

The badge URL is https://defiscoring.com/badge/<wallet>.svg. We render the latest persisted score from our scoring engine; if the wallet hasn't been scanned yet, the badge shows "no scan yet" until one is captured. The image is cached for 5 minutes at the edge so embeds stay fast.
