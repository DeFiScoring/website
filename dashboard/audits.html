About this directory

Audit reports are one of the strongest off-chain signals we use when scoring a protocol's Security factor. This page lists the firms whose reports we ingest, their areas of expertise, and a sample of recent reports for protocols tracked by DeFi Scoring. All links point to publicly available reports on the firm's own infrastructure — we don't mirror them. If you spot a missing report, file it through Report an Issue.

How DeFi Scoring uses audits

Tier weighting

A Tier-1 firm audit contributes more to the Security score than an unverified or single-engineer review.

Recency decay

Reports older than 18 months are discounted; protocol surface area changes faster than that.

Findings status

Open critical or high findings cap the Security factor regardless of how many other audits exist.

Diversity bonus

Independent audits from two or more firms raise confidence and the floor of the Security factor.
