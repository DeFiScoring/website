How the score is built

The score combines on-chain signals weighted as shown above: payment history (no liquidations in the last 90 days), utilization (borrowed vs supplied), account history length, activity diversity, and fraud / sybil signals. Phase 1 covers Ethereum, Arbitrum, and Polygon. Phase 2 will add Optimism and Base.
