Before we start

Enter your email and the chatbot will ask 5–7 short questions about your DeFi goals, experience and risk tolerance. At the end you'll get a personalised risk-profile summary and can download it as a PDF.

We store your email so we can let you know when new audit reports are published. Conversation transcripts live in cache only and are deleted automatically after one hour. We never share email addresses.
