Wallet risk exposure scanner

Connect a wallet to detect every protocol you've interacted with on Ethereum, Arbitrum, and Polygon. Each row shows the protocol's live composite score — interactions with protocols below band 60 are highlighted in red so you can review them first. Scans run on the Cloudflare edge and are cached per-wallet for 10 minutes.
