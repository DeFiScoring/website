Why this matters

Every time you swap or supply a token, the protocol asks for an allowance — permission to pull that token from your wallet. Wallets default to unlimited grants for convenience. If a router or pool gets exploited later, every wallet still holding an unlimited approval is drained at the same time. The scan below flags those grants so you can rescind them on revoke.cash.
