Account & Privacy

DeFiScoring is a read-only analytics tool. There are no user accounts to log into — these controls operate on the wallet address you choose to give us.

Privacy preferences Live

Manage what telemetry is collected when you use the dashboard, and how long it's kept.

Open privacy preferences →

Export my data (DSAR) Live

Download every row in our database that references your wallet — health scores, watchlists, community votes, anonymized telemetry. The export is a JSON file you can save or share with a privacy regulator.

Connect your wallet on the dashboard, then call:

GET /api/account/export?address=0xYourAddress

The signed-link-via-email variant ships once SIWE wallet sign-in lands.

Delete my data (DSAR) Phase 2

Soft-delete with a 30-day purge window requires signed proof of wallet ownership (SIWE) — otherwise anyone could delete anyone else's data. Until that ships, we honor manual deletion requests by email:

privacy@defiscoring.com (please send from a wallet you control, or include a signed message)

Data retention

Raw event rows are auto-pruned after 180 days by a nightly job. Aggregated, non-identifying rollups are kept indefinitely so we can plot long-term trends without storing your activity.
