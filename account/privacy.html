Privacy preferences

All settings persist in your browser's localStorage and apply immediately. Nothing is sent to our servers when you toggle them.

Anonymized usage telemetry off saved

Send anonymized event pings (score render, profiler run, approvals scan) so we can publish aggregate market-intel charts. Your wallet address is hashed (sha256 + HMAC) before it ever reaches our database.

Storage key: defi:intel:consent

Share anonymized wallet snapshots with research off saved

Optional: share periodic anonymized portfolio snapshots (token mix, position sizes — never the address) with the DeFiScoring research team. Default off. Revoking triggers a delete job that purges your snapshots within 24 hours.

Storage key: defi:research:consent · the snapshot table itself ships with the admin SPA in Phase 3 — toggle is wired now so consent is captured from day one.
Your rights: You can request a full export of any data tied to your wallet at any time via GET /api/account/export?address=0x…, or by emailing privacy@defiscoring.com. Deletion requests are honored within 30 days; until SIWE wallet sign-in ships in Phase 2, deletion requests must be sent by email and signed.
